Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This policy covers the ConText Editor Chrome extension and the ConText Editor website. Your notes stay on your device — we do not collect them.

1. Data Collection

We collect no personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, and no sync.

  • No analytics, tracking, cookies, or fingerprinting
  • No ads
  • No selling or sharing of your data

2. Local Storage

Your notes are stored only in your browser on this device. They work offline and never leave your computer as part of how this app is built.

The Chrome extension only opens or focuses the editor tab. It does not read other websites, and it does not see or send your notes. The website host serves the app files only — not your notes.

3. Data Deletion

  • Delete a note in the editor anytime.
  • Clear all notes by clearing this site’s data in your browser settings, or by uninstalling the extension (that does not remove notes stored by the website — clear site data for that).

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

Because we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to access, export, or delete on your behalf. You control everything that lives in your browser.
